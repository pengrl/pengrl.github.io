RWA Asset

After each piece of artwork is mapped through the CATALOGUE System, it will circulate on the blockchain in the form of RWA assets. Similar to the artwork itself, RWAs possess unique attributes and are uniquely tied to high-value artworks. Transaction earnings generated by each physical artwork will be distributed as profit-sharing rights to the corresponding RWA asset holders.

Buyers can conveniently purchase/sell the RWA assets associated with the artwork online at any time, representing a transaction or transfer of ownership of the artwork itself. In other words, RWA is bound to the artwork in a unique mapping relationship.

BeArt will provide comprehensive and convenient physical artwork delivery services to users who purchase RWA Collections, including but not limited to in-person delivery, shipping, custody, or even destruction, all of which will undergo verification before any form of delivery to ensure the protection of user rights.

RWA assets also possess NFT-Fi attributes (RWAD), featuring unlimited/limited fractionalization financial derivative gameplay. Similar to artwork options or futures, users can subscribe to the 1/N equity certificate of the RWA and obtain corresponding 1/N asset ownership and profit-sharing rights. For specific details, refer to NFT fractionalization gameplay. It’s important to note that only holders of 100% of the corresponding RWA asset equity certificate can redeem the complete RWA asset and enjoy all associated rights. If holding only a portion, they do not own the physical asset.

BeArt will exclusively issue a series of RWA assets based on the concept of 100% real-world mapping, and the mapping standards generated by the CATALOGUE System will support various on-chain trading platforms.
