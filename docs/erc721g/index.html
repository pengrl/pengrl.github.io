ERC721g

  • Collector Information Tag: Provides detailed personal or institutional information about the art owner

  • Collection Details: Includes basic information about the artwork such as name, creator, creation year, material, and any special historical or narrative aspects.

  • Scientific Analysis Data: Utilizes advanced technology to scientifically analyze the artwork, recording critical data such as material composition and preservation condition.

  • Additional Auxiliary Information: Any supplementary information helpful for enhancing the value and understanding of the artwork.

These new tags and modules enable the ERC721g standard to offer richer and more nuanced information descriptions, meeting the precision and complexity demands of different scenarios for art-related information.

The ERC721g standard supports unlimited audits of art-related information (both on-chain and off-chain). Since all data is recorded on the blockchain network, any user can verify information about art derivatives, including ownership history, transfer records, and authenticity proofs. This significantly enhances transparency and trust in art assets.
