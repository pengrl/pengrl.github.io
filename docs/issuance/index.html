BeArt Token Issuance

BeArt Token Distribution:

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 (1 billion tokens) BeArt Tokens.
  • Issuance Mechanism: No issuance, continuous deflation.

The distribution of BeArt Tokens is divided into three main categories:

  • Community Ecosystem Incentives: Used for various incentives in BeArt community ecosystem development, including Ecosystem Mining Pools (MP), Public Offerings (PO), and Strategic Investments (Investment), aiming to reward all participants in ecosystem development roles.
  • Project Operations (OC): Mainly for the initial development of the project, including research and development, product development, operations, marketing, and community management.
  • Ecosystem Incentives: Mainly includes Regular Dividend Rewards (DRP) and Governance Treasury (GV), converting profit generated during ecosystem operation into incentives distributed back to all users, achieving a positive feedback loop. The dividend reward pool will continuously receive 10% to 15% of BeArt Tokens from ecosystem repurchases as supplements (not exceeding the initial limit).
