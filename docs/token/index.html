Flow Scenarios of BeArt Token

The circulation of BeArt Token will be reflected in the following scenarios:

  • Payment for ecosystem services, including but not limited to various professional services provided by BeArt.
  • Payment of transaction commissions within the ecosystem, such as commissions for NFTs, RWA, and art derivatives transactions.
  • Payment of annual subscription fees for platform VIP services, including BeArt VIP annual subscription fees.
  • Payment for future GAS fees and platform application staking on the self-owned blockchain network.
