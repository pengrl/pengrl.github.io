What is BeArt?

Through the integration of technology and financial innovation, BeArt has mapped real-world art assets into the crypto domain. With proprietary detection technology patents, BeArt introduces RWA assets that resemble NFTs but are based on protocols similar to ERC721 and ERC1155, along with associated derivative assets known as RWAD.

This marks a significant departure from traditional NFT art realms. BeArt utilizes a proprietary patented technology rooted in the value of real art assets and a 100% mapping standard. While similar in form to NFTs, RWA uses different blockchain technology standards, distinguishing it from NFTs and providing comprehensive mapping of real art assets.

Despite the previous NFT boom, the crypto realm has struggled to bridge the gap between encrypted assets and real assets. The emergence of the Metaverse offered a glimpse of hope, yet people do not seek a mere replica of the real world. Although NFT art garnered significant attention, it mostly focused on avant-garde artistic expression, lacking ties to real-world assets.

Art assets themselves carry added value due to cultural, religious, and aesthetic factors, making them significant investment assets with high market entry barriers. Consequently, derivative products in this field primarily consist of various forms of secondary creative goods.

Blockchain technology innovations, such as perpetual storage, decentralization, and transaction circulation, naturally lend themselves as forms of real art derivative carriers. Therefore, utilizing NFTs as a proven mature form of encrypted assets to map real art assets is a suitable approach. BeArt innovates on mainstream NFT protocol standards through innovative art collection techniques, resulting in RWA encrypted assets.

While RWA assets bear similarity to NFTs, they have yet to become mainstream encrypted asset categories. Leading art auction houses like Christie’s are also exploring RWA-related standards, but the consensus on this encrypted asset standard is still developing, with BeArt actively contributing to this progress.

The application of RWA assets will address the recognition and circulation issues of art derivatives in the crypto market. BeArt, through its official NFT and RWA asset trading platform, facilitates interaction between traditional art markets and the crypto market, ushering in new growth directions and driving forces for the art market.
