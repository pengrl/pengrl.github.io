Roadmap

Past Achievements:

  • 2018: BeArt Web3 project officially launched, and the core team was formed.
  • 2019: BeArt strategic direction and technical roadmap were established, and technical research and development commenced. The CATALOGUE System was under development, exploring the implementation logic of Genetic NFT standards and determining on-chain technical solutions.
  • 2020: Experimental-level demo completed under the Genetic standard.
  • 2021: Design of BeArt Web3 products and economic model; MVP verification of CATALOGUE System completed; Commercial-level demo under the Genetic standard finished. Design work on dWeb 1.0 version and BeArt dApp 1.0 version also initiated.
  • 2022: BeArt Gallery dWeb 1.0 version underwent internal testing; BeArt Gallery dWeb 2.0 version commenced internal testing; BeArt dApp 1.0 version began internal testing. Initial partnership with BeArt Spaces achieved, collaborating with leading global university labs to advance the BeArt Genetic NFT standard and establish the Tianlu Authentication System.
  • 2023: Completion of RWA Collection product demo design; on-chain data testing completed; BVC NFT design work finalized. Collaboration with international artists and collectors commenced, collecting top-tier artworks across various categories for RWA production. Simultaneously, BeArt dWeb 3.0 version entered internal testing.

Starting from 2024, BeArt embarked on a rapid market expansion phase:

  • 2024, Q1: BeArt Website V4.0 launched for public beta testing, completing seed round financing. Continual updates and enhancements to the website functionality, enabling on-chain minting for RWA Collection.
  • 2024, Q2: Release of BeArt Whitepaper, officially launching marketing campaigns and promotions. Completion of angel round financing and launch of RWA Gallery, alongside the release of the Genesis NFT series. Additionally, the Auction Center feature was officially (test-run) launched, allowing users to bid on preferred RWA assets.
  • 2024, Q3: Commencement of BeArt Token airdrops and entitlement phase. 2024, Q4: BeArt Token TGE (Token Generation Event) launched, and the BeArt platform officially supported Token trading, consumption, and circulation. The public beta version of BeArt dApp and Artverse functionality was released.
